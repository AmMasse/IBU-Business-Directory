📋 Installation Instructions for Android:

  1. Go to Settings → Security
  2. Enable "Install from unknown sources"
  3. Open the downloaded APK file
  4. Follow the installation prompts
  5. Accept the required permissions

Note: The app requires certain permissions to function properly, including location access to show nearby businesses.

🧠 How to Use the App

👤 As a Guest User

You can use Mr. IBU as a guest and simply ask our AI assistant what you're looking for. No registration required to search for businesses and professionals in your area.

Example: "I need a plumber near me" or "Show me the best restaurants in my area"

🏢 As a Business Owner

Sign up for a free account and add up to 3 businesses to the directory. Once signed in, go to "My Business Dashboard" to manage your listings.

Steps:

  1. Create your account
  2. Access "My Business Dashboard"
  3. Fill in your business details
  4. Submit for verification

✅ Verification Process

After submitting your business details, go to the "About Us" section in the app to communicate with our team. Every business has different verification standards based on the industry and services offered.

Note: Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your business type.
